Metal Additive Technologies

01

DMLS — Laser Sintering

Direct Metal Laser Sintering: 20-60μm layers, 99.5% density, ideal for complex internal geometries. Best alloys: AlSi10Mg, Ti6Al4V, 17-4 PH, Inconel 718.

02

SLM — Full Melting

Selective Laser Melting achieves 100% melt pool fusion. Superior for 316L stainless and CoCr biomedical applications requiring maximum material homogeneity.

03

DED — Wire Arc

Directed Energy Deposition for large structural components and repair applications. Wire arc additive manufacturing for titanium aerospace structures at scale.
